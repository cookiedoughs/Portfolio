Fitbit Smart Device Case Study

BellaBeat is a small company that manufactures health-focused products primarily for women. The goal for this project is to understand trends on how consumers are using their non-Bellabeat smart devices, specifically FitBit data from Kaggle. This will help the marketing team to develop marketing strategies for their products geared towards women.
